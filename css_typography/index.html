Ayodhya Bar Association Decision in Donation Case.





In Ayodhya, the Ram Mandir has come under attention after allegations of irregularities in donation management. Authorities have taken strict action, and multiple people connected to the case have been arrested for investigation. According to reports, a special investigation team (SIT) was formed to look into the matter after complaints about missing or misused donations given by devotees for the construction and maintenance of the temple. The case has now reached the courts, where the Supreme Court refused urgent hearing of a petition demanding a CBI investigation, saying the matter will follow normal legal procedures. Meanwhile, several individuals have already been sent to judicial custody, and the temple trust has assured devotees that all offerings and valuables are safe and properly recorded. This issue has also triggered political reactions, with different parties commenting on the management and transparency of temple funds.
